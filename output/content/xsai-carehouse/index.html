Xsai CareHome

Manage your ROTA Effectively and Go Paperless and Mobile.

Managing a care home is amazingly rewarding, but it can be difficult to know how best to organise your time between employees, service users and healthcare professionals. Through our Enterprise Resource Planning, we can work together to create a management resource that can solve your problems and help you streamline the productivity of your business.

As technology has evolved, we have been able to create systems that allow you to get everything you need in one place. This means being able to store all relevant documents, care plans, ROTAs, schedules and HR resources. We can build a platform that not only does this, but creates a live record detailing the care and activities of your service users. Instead of sifting through folders of paperwork, calendars, employee records and having to keep important meetings and referrals in your head or a paper diary that can be forgotten, we can put all of this information in an easily accessible online platform. In doing so, we can free you of time-consuming HR and administrative tasks to help you focus on providing an excellent level of care to your service users.

How it works?

We can create a secure online platform that allows you to access a centralised Cloud database to manage your documentation. Through this platform we can then: Manage care plans, activities planning and ROTAs to provide a smooth-running schedule for your care home.

Features

Our software will incorporate the features your business needs in an intuitive, easy to use design with custom dashboards to help you access the data you need as quickly as possible. Once this has been set up, you can access it through a web portal as well as a mobile app that works on both Android and iOS platforms.

Key Features

  • You can schedule ROTAs and tasks, allowing caregivers to see both their daily and weekly work tasks to help them plan the day.
  • You can automate timesheets, payroll and invoicing to make the HR process as quick as possible for you and your employees.
  • Service users can access the platform to view their care plans, activities planners and to track their progress. This is incredibly useful to supply you with up-to-date information to help assess their care package if their needs change.
  • The overview of the work being done by your care assistants can be easily accessed, making it easy to identify areas for improvement and further training, and also to celebrate exceptional work. The system will also allow extensive reporting on the day-to-day activities of your business.
