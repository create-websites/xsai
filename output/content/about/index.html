About Us

We are a young and creative microsoft power BI consultancy in UK and we offer consultancy services in Business Intelligence, Data Integration and Office 365 Solutions.

Who we are?
Welcome to Xsai Consulting. We are a software consultancy company based in London, UK. We specialise in providing Microsoft Power BI consultancy services, Data Integration services and Excel-based services.

If you need something more tailored to you, we provide custom web development using Microsoft ASP.NET MVC Core. We can also work on custom mobile app development for iOS and Android platforms to make it as easy as possible for you to access the software that will propel your business forward. We can support development in both native and cross-platform enhancement tool Xamarin. The key products we offer are in Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP). These include:

– ERP for Facility Management (Xsai-FM)
– ERP for Care Homes (Xsai-Carehome)
– ERP for Distribution (Xsai-Distribution)

These three industries often suffer from the same pain points within HR management and the handling of large amounts of logistical data that needs to be accessed and managed in real-time. We know how overwhelming a workload like this can become using more traditional record-keeping techniques, and we do not want paperwork to tie you down when one of our efficient paperless systems can save you the headache. Our ERP products are ideal for small to medium scale businesses. They are SaaS-based solutions that are hosted securely on the Cloud.
