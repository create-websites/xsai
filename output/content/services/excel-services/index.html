Excel Services

Xsai Consulting provides excel based consultancy services in UK. We can help you with all types of Excel development and Office Automation Projects. We can convert your static Excel Templates into Smart Dynamic fully functional applications.

How we can help you

Hire excel VBA consultants & VSTO office automation consultants now. We can help you with Microsoft Office Automation and Excel requirements using VBA/Microsoft VSTO.

Microsoft Excel or Google Worksheets are most commonly used by all Accountants, Finance Administrators and CFOs. Also Sales professionals use Microsoft Excel for planning, budgeting and forecasting. They build complex Excel templates with lots of complicated formulas and even use Macros. The templates used are static and shared with team members by email. Since templates are static or in some cases maintained by individual team members they are often out of sync and employees spend long hours consolidating data from different excel sheets. Our expert Excel consultants can help you support your existing VBA based applications or convert your static templates into sophisticated dynamic Microsoft Excel based applications so all your data is centrally stored in a secure back-end database either on-premise or in the cloud. This can enable you and your colleagues access data without worrying about maintaining multiple versions of Excel templates. You can focus on your core business functionality and day to day operations instead of spending time maintaining static excel templates.
