Facility Management Reimagined/ Go Mobile /Go Paperless

Let us redefine facility management together. Our Enterprise Resource Planning can solve all your problems, whether you are a landlord, a tenant or an estate agent.

Imagine having one single space to manage your entire portfolio. It contains all your documents, communication, task lists, schedules, purchase orders and inventory data. No need to rifle through piles of paperwork, search your email inbox and field calls while trying to keep a busy schedule in a paper diary or even your head. All of this is possible and we want to help you simplify your resources so you have more time and energy to focus on the tasks that will expand your business.

How it works?

We can provide a secure online portal and mobile app to manage your real estate assets. Plus points of this platform are as follows:

Easy configuration and Setup
Connect everyone related to a project (landlords, tenants, real estate agents and contractors)
Store every document and communication related to the project on a central and secure Cloud database
Set up and configure your master data, set up users and security roles
Manage your schedule with handy appointment reminders

Features

Our software will be intentionally designed to include the features you want and need for your business without an overly complicated user interface or setup. We will create an intuitive platform that is quick to set up and easy to engage with once you go live. Once set up you can access the platform online and through a mobile app available on both Android and iOS systems.

Key Features

  • You will be able to schedule and manage technician appointments, assigning both family and weekly tasks to be completed.
  • You can effectively manage both reactive and preventive maintenance issues with real-time resource visibility at your fingertips.
  • You can manage your relationship with your tenants. They can register a complaint online which you can respond to and solve using the platform to assess how best to inspect the property or contact contractors with work that needs to be carried out.
  • You can create purchase orders, manage inventory across locations and generate extensive purchase and inventory reports .

Tech Stack Used

  • ASP.NET MVC Core
  • C#
  • Microsoft .NET
  • Inhouse designed and easy to configure ERP Framework
